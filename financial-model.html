Overview

Project Background

This project involved building a fully integrated 3-statement financial model for Spotify Technology S.A. — one of the world's leading audio streaming platforms. The objective was to produce a robust, dynamic model that could be used for investment analysis, scenario planning and strategic decision-making.

Spotify operates on a freemium model, generating revenue through premium subscriptions and advertising. Understanding the interplay between subscriber growth, ARPU and cost structure was central to building a credible model.

"The goal was not just to build a model — but to tell the story of how Spotify makes money and where it is going."

Methodology

How the Model Was Built

  • 01
    Revenue Build-Up Modelled subscriber growth by region, average revenue per user (ARPU) and advertising revenue separately to reflect Spotify's dual revenue streams.
  • 02
    Cost Structure Broke down cost of revenue, R&D, sales & marketing and G&A with historical trend analysis and forward-looking assumptions tied to revenue growth.
  • 03
    3-Statement Integration Linked P&L, Balance Sheet and Cash Flow statement so any assumption change flows automatically through the entire model.
  • 04
    Scenario Analysis Built dynamic scenario toggles for Bull, Base and Bear cases with sensitivity tables showing impact on EBITDA and Free Cash Flow.
  • 05
    Sanity Checks Validated all outputs against sell-side consensus estimates and historical actuals to ensure the model was credible and presentation-ready.
